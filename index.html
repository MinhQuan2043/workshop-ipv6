<p># Workshop: Trin khai v Chuyn i IPv6 trn AWS</p>
<p>---</p>
<p>## 1. Gii thiu v IPv6</p>
<p>### 1.1 IPv6 l g v ti sao cn chuyn i?</p>
<ul>
<li>IPv6 (Internet Protocol version 6) l th h tip theo ca giao thc
Internet, c thit k thay th IPv4.</li>
<li>L do chnh cho s chuyn i l do **s cn kit a ch IPv4**.
IPv4 ch c khong 4.3 t a ch, trong khi IPv6 cung cp mt s lng
a ch khng l: $3.4 \times 10^{38}$.</li>
</ul>
<p>### 1.2 So snh IPv4 v IPv6</p>
<ul>
<li>**nh dng a ch**: IPv4 s dng 32-bit (v d:
<code>192.168.1.1</code>), cn IPv6 s dng 128-bit (v d:
<code>2001:0db8:85a3:0000:0000:8a2e:0370:7334</code>).</li>
<li>**Bo mt**: IPsec l ty chn trn IPv4, nhng l **bt buc** v
c tch hp sn trong giao thc IPv6, cung cp m ha v xc thc u
cui.</li>
<li>**Cu hnh**: IPv6 h tr **SLAAC (Stateless Address
Autoconfiguration)**, cho php cc thit b t cu hnh a ch m khng
cn my ch DHCPv6.</li>
</ul>
<p>---</p>
<p>## 2. Chin lc Trin khai v Di chuyn IPv6</p>
<p>### 2.1 Cc phng php di chuyn</p>
<ul>
<li>**Dual-stack**: Trin khai c IPv4 v IPv6 cng lc. y l phng
php ph bin nht trn m my.</li>
<li>**Tunneling**: ng gi cc gi tin IPv6 bn trong cc gi tin IPv4
truyn qua mng IPv4.</li>
<li>**NAT64/DNS64**: Cho php cc my ch ch h tr IPv6 giao tip vi
cc my ch ch h tr IPv4.</li>
</ul>
<p>### 2.2 Chin lc Dual-stack trn AWS</p>
<p>Chng ta s s dng chin lc Dual-stack trn AWS m bo tnh
tng thch vi cc h thng c trong khi vn tn dng c li ch ca
IPv6.</p>
<p>---</p>
<p>## 3. Thc hnh trn AWS: Trin khai Dual-stack</p>
<p>y l phn hng dn tng bc. Bn s thc hin theo cc bc ny
trn ti khon AWS ca mnh.</p>
<p>### 3.1 Bc 1: To VPC v Subnet</p>
<ul>
<li>Vo giao din qun l AWS, tm kim **VPC**.</li>
<li>Nhn **Create VPC**.</li>
<li>**Cu hnh CIDR IPv4**: Chn mt di CIDR nh
<code>10.0.0.0/16</code>.</li>
<li>**Cu hnh CIDR IPv6**: AWS s t ng cung cp mt di a ch IPv6
($/56$) cho VPC ca bn.</li>
<li>To 2 Subnet trong VPC ny, mi Subnet s c t ng gn mt di
a ch IPv6 ($/64$).</li>
</ul>
<p>### 3.2 Bc 2: Cp nht Bng nh tuyn (Route Table)</p>
<ul>
<li>To mt **Internet Gateway (IGW)** v gn n vo VPC ca bn.</li>
<li>Cp nht bng nh tuyn chnh ca VPC:</li>
<li>Thm mt route mi cho IPv4: <code>0.0.0.0/0</code> tr ti IGW.</li>
<li>Thm mt route mi cho IPv6: <code>::/0</code> tr ti IGW.</li>
</ul>
<p>### 3.3 Bc 3: Cu hnh Security Group v EC2</p>
<ul>
<li>To mt Security Group cho php truy cp HTTP v SSH t c IPv4 v
IPv6.</li>
<li>Khi chy mt **EC2 Instance** (v d: Amazon Linux 2 AMI) trong
Subnet to.</li>
<li>m bo <code>Auto-assign public IPv4 address</code> v <code>Auto-
assign IPv6 address</code> u c bt.</li>
</ul>
